Kabuli Pulao

Description

Kabuli Pulao is a traditional Afghan rice dish made with tender lamb or chicken, basmati rice, and a delightful mix of sweet and savory flavors. The dish is garnished with caramelized carrots, raisins, and slivered almonds, which add a delicious contrast to the seasoned meat and fluffy rice. A hint of cardamom and cinnamon gives this dish its unique and aromatic flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Lamb or Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Raisins
  • Slivered Almonds
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Yogurt
  • Cardamom
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Cumin
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Vegetable Oil
