Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, tender marinated meat (chicken, mutton, or beef), and a blend of fragrant spices including saffron, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaves. This dish is slow-cooked to perfection to infuse every grain of rice with rich flavors, creating a mouth-watering experience.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken/Mutton/Beef
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger and Garlic Paste
  • Yogurt
  • Saffron
  • Cardamom
  • Cloves
  • Bay Leaves
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Fresh Coriander and Mint Leaves
