Chole Bhature

Description

Chole Bhature is a classic North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas (Chole) served with deep-fried bread (Bhature). This hearty and flavorful dish is known for its rich, tangy, and spicy flavors that come from a blend of aromatic spices and a hint of tanginess from tomatoes and lemon juice. It is often enjoyed as a popular breakfast or lunch dish.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (Chole)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green Chilies
  • Spices (Cumin, Coriander, Garam Masala, Turmeric, Red Chili Powder)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Flour (for Bhature)
  • Yogurt
  • Baking Soda
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
