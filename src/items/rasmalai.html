Rasmalai

Description

Rasmalai is a delectable Indian dessert made of soft paneer (cottage cheese) patties soaked in sweetened, flavored milk. The dish is garnished with a mix of chopped nuts like pistachios and almonds, and delicately flavored with cardamom and saffron, giving it a rich, creamy texture and an irresistible aroma.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Cottage Cheese)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Saffron
  • Chopped Pistachios
  • Chopped Almonds
  • Rose Water
  • Citric Acid or Lemon Juice (for curdling milk)
